Our Story

Tanzania's Trusted Poultry Farm Since 2016

Founded in Tabata Sundown, Dar es Salaam, Nico Poultry & Eggs Ltd began with one mission: deliver the freshest, most ethically raised poultry to Tanzanian families and businesses.

Today we serve over 100 satisfied customers — from private households to Dar es Salaam's finest restaurants and hotels — with the same commitment to quality.

Ethically Raised
All Turkey and Chickens raised open-range with organic feed — no hormones, no antibiotics, ever.
Cold-Chain Delivery
Temperature-controlled transport ensures every product arrives fresh and safe within 24 hours.
Wholesale Partnerships
Custom supply agreements for hotels, restaurants, supermarkets and event caterers.
